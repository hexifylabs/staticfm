Built for Radio Fans

Every feature is designed around one goal — getting you to your music, talk shows, and live stations as fast and seamlessly as possible.

📡
Live Streaming
Tune into thousands of live internet radio stations from around the world — any genre, any language, any time.
🎵
Live Metadata
See exactly what's playing in real time — song title, artist, and album art update automatically as each track changes.
📱
Home Screen Widgets
Control playback without ever opening the app. Beautiful, glanceable widgets put your station right on your home screen.
📺
Chromecast Support
Cast your favorite stations to any Chromecast-enabled speaker, TV, or display. Your phone stays free while the music fills the room.
🔁
Background Playback
StaticFM keeps playing even when you switch apps, lock your screen, or take a call. Uninterrupted radio, always.
🔍
Station Discovery
Browse and search a massive directory of stations by genre, country, or keyword. Find something new to love every day.
⭐
Favorites
Save your go-to stations for instant one-tap access. Your lineup, always ready when you are.
🎛️
Media Controls
Full notification controls, lock screen integration, and Bluetooth headset support — radio on your terms.
